About BlockDrop

BlockDrop is a classic block-stacking puzzle game. Clear full rows to score points and keep the board from filling up.

How to play:

  • Use arrow keys to move pieces left and right
  • Press Down to soft drop
  • Press Up or Space to rotate
  • Press Enter to pause or restart

BlockDrop is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No installation required—just play in your browser.
