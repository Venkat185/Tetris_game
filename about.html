About BlockDrop

BlockDrop is a classic block-stacking puzzle game. Clear full rows to score points and keep the board from filling up. Play free in your browser—no installation required.

Objective

Your goal is to clear as many horizontal lines as possible by filling them completely with blocks. Each cleared line disappears and adds to your score. The game ends when new blocks can no longer spawn at the top of the board.

Controls & Scores

Controls:

  • ← → Move piece left or right
  • ↓ Soft drop (move down faster)
  • ↑ or Space Rotate piece clockwise
  • C Hold current piece for later use
  • Enter Pause or resume the game

Scoring:

  • 100 points per line × current level
  • Level increases every 10 lines cleared

Gameplay Info

Use the HOLD panel to store a piece and the NEXT panel to see the upcoming piece. Plan your moves ahead to clear multiple lines at once and maximize your score!

BlockDrop is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No installation required—just play in your browser.
