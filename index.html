BlockDrop © 2026. A classic block-stacking puzzle game. Play free in your browser.

How to Play

Objective: Clear full rows by filling them with blocks. Each cleared row adds to your score.

Controls:

  • ← → Move left/right
  • ↓ Soft drop
  • ↑ or Space Rotate
  • Enter Pause / Resume

Hold: Press C to hold the current piece for later use.
